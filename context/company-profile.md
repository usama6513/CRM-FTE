# TechCorp SaaS Company Profile

## Overview
TechCorp is a mid-sized SaaS company that provides a comprehensive customer relationship management platform. Founded in 2018, the company has grown to serve over 50,000 customers worldwide.

## Products
- TechCorp CRM Pro: Advanced customer relationship management for growing businesses
- TechCorp Analytics: Business intelligence and reporting platform
- TechCorp Integrations: Suite of third-party integration tools

## Customer Base
- Primary: Small to medium businesses (5-500 employees)
- Secondary: Enterprise clients with 500+ employees
- Customer demographics: Tech-savvy professionals in sales, marketing, and customer success

## Support Volume
- Average daily inquiries: 500-800
- Peak hours: 9 AM - 5 PM EST (Monday-Friday)
- Common issues: API integration, feature requests, billing questions, technical troubleshooting

## Company Values
- Customer-first approach
- Data security and privacy
- Innovation and continuous improvement
- Responsive and helpful support
